Cross-Platform Prediction Arbitrage

Revenue 24/7 Low Risk
Intermediate

The same event is listed on Polymarket, Kalshi, Manifold, and PredictIt ‚Äî often at different prices. Buy "Yes" on one platform where it's cheap, buy "No" on another where it's cheap. Guaranteed profit if the prices are far enough apart.

How it works:
1. Scrape/API-poll the same markets across 3-4 platforms
2. LLM matches equivalent markets across platforms (different titles, same event)
3. Calculate arbitrage spread: if Platform A says 55% and Platform B says 40% for the same event ‚Üí buy Yes on B, buy No on A
4. Alert or auto-execute when spread > fees
5. No prediction needed ‚Äî pure math arbitrage

Edge: The LLM's job is matching markets across platforms (they use different titles/descriptions). The actual arb is just math. Low risk because you're hedged on both sides.

Potential: $20-200/month (limited by liquidity and arb opportunities)
Polymarket API Kalshi API Manifold API gemma3:4b (market matching)

ATS Resume Rewriter & Cover Letter Generator

Career Easy Run Now
Beginner

Feed it your master resume + a job posting URL. It rewrites your resume to beat ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems), match keywords, and generates a custom cover letter ‚Äî all locally, all free. You can build this right now on your Mac before the Jetson even arrives.

How ATS works (and how to beat it):
  - Companies use ATS software (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever) to auto-filter resumes
  - ATS scores your resume by keyword match against the job description
  - If your resume doesn't hit ~70% keyword match, a human never sees it
  - Most people send the same resume everywhere and get filtered out

What the bot does:

1. Parse Your Master Resume:
  - Store your full work history, skills, projects, education in structured JSON
  - This is your "source of truth" ‚Äî every real thing you've done
  - Update it once, generate infinite tailored versions

2. Analyze the Job Posting:
  - Paste a URL or job description
  - LLM extracts: required skills, nice-to-haves, keywords, tone, seniority level
  - Identifies ATS keywords: exact phrases like "CI/CD", "cross-functional", "stakeholder management"
  - Detects the company's language style (startup casual vs enterprise formal)

3. Rewrite Resume for THIS Job:
  - Maps your experience to their requirements (which of your projects match which requirement)
  - Rewrites bullet points using THEIR keywords naturally (not keyword stuffing)
  - Reorders sections to put most relevant experience first
  - Adjusts your title/summary to mirror their job title
  - Quantifies results: "improved performance" ‚Üí "reduced API latency by 40%"
  - Keeps it honest ‚Äî only rephrases what you actually did, never fabricates

4. Generate Cover Letter:
  - References the specific company, role, and team
  - Connects your experience to their mission/product
  - Mentions something specific about the company (recent news, product launch)
  - Matches their tone (startup energy vs corporate professional)
  - 3 paragraphs: hook ‚Üí why I'm a fit ‚Üí call to action

5. ATS Score Check:
  - Compares your tailored resume against the job description
  - Reports keyword match percentage
  - Flags missing keywords you could add
  - Checks formatting (ATS hates tables, columns, headers, fancy fonts)
  - Ensures clean plain-text parsing (what ATS actually sees vs what you see)

6. Output:
  - Clean PDF resume (ATS-friendly formatting)
  - Cover letter PDF
  - ATS score report
  - Suggested LinkedIn message to the recruiter for this role

The Claude Projects angle: You can set this up as a Claude Project right now ‚Äî upload your master resume as project context, then for each job posting just paste the description and ask it to tailor. But that costs API credits per use. The local version on Ollama/Jetson = unlimited rewrites for free. Apply to 50 jobs a day, each with a custom resume, zero cost.

Real talk: The difference between "applied with generic resume" and "applied with ATS-optimized tailored resume + personalized cover letter" is the difference between getting ghosted and getting interviews. Most people are too lazy to customize per job. You automate it.

Potential: Land interviews 3-5x faster. Could also sell as a service ‚Äî $20-50 per resume tailoring.
